Who we are

Philosophy
and History.

Our discreet management supports artists who stand in the light — globally connected, always in the background.

Those who stand in the light deserve a place of complete shadow.

— Founding principle, Fips Flake

Our
Philosophy
—
Stance

Invisibility as strength.

Most consultancies thrive on visibility — awards, client lists, industry prizes. At Fips Flake, the opposite holds: our quality is measured by the fact that nobody knows we were involved.

We do not appear in public, do not advertise and forgo industry engagements. Where we maintain a presence on social networks, this is deliberate, selective and exclusively in the interest of our clients. This website is our only official statement — and it says less than it conceals.

Clientele

Only for individuals.

We do not advise companies, political figures or institutions. Our mandate applies exclusively to artists as individuals.

This restriction is not a marketing strategy — it is an ethical decision. Artists are particularly vulnerable in their public exposure. Taking this protection seriously means making it the sole purpose.

Method

No noise. Only effect.

Our work is not built on campaigns, press releases or public statements. It is built on thorough analysis, long-term planning and the willingness to act in quiet moments — before a problem becomes visible.

Every intervention, every recommendation, every ghostwriting project follows a single criterion: does it serve the person, or does it serve the narrative? We always choose the person.

Mental Health

Person first. Always.

Given the growing media presence and public pressure that artists increasingly face, clients can also access therapeutic support through Fips Flake.

Only carefully selected mental coaches and qualified therapists are referred — thoroughly vetted, discreetly engaged and subject to the same confidentiality obligations as all other collaborators.

This service is not an add-on. It is part of our core understanding: protecting a career means protecting the person behind it.

Confidentiality

Agreements that apply for life.

Each client receives a dedicated team member tailored specifically to their needs. The number of collaborators is deliberately and strictly limited — to ensure quality, trust and undivided attention.

All collaborators and external contractors sign confidentiality agreements of unlimited duration. They commit to silence even after a mandate ends — and after their work with Fips Flake concludes. Not only about content. Also about names. Also about existence.

These agreements are legally binding and any breach is subject to legal prosecution. Violations of confidentiality obligations are pursued consistently and without exception.

History
1980s
The Founder — a Career, a Decision

The founder of Fips Flake is an artist himself — with a career spanning acting, theatre, directing, dubbing, media production and music. From his own experience in the public eye grew the understanding of what artists truly need: protection, discretion and someone who knows what is at stake. The founder remains out of sight — just as he ensures his clients do.

2000s
Founded — Early 2000s

Born from years of firsthand experience, Fips Flake is formally founded — to give others what the founder himself had lacked. Active as a collaborator at the intersection of film production, music marketing and strategic consultancy. Key stations and co-productions:

  • —Co-involvement in productions with actors from the USA
  • —Co-productions, Studios Babelsberg
  • —Involvement with ProSieben / Sat.1, Munich
  • —Long-standing involvement with UFA
  • —Co-productions for RTL
2010s
Discreet Managing

The service profile is formalised and grows. Every collaboration begins with bespoke confidentiality agreements. Involvements and co-productions with artists from, among others:

  • —Germany
  • —France
  • —USA
  • —Canada
2020s
Pause — Operations Reduced to a Minimum

In the early 2020s, operations had to be scaled back to a minimum for several years. No new mandates, no active growth — a deliberate decision to preserve quality and integrity. Since early 2025, former structures have been gradually reinstated.

2025
Renewed Activity and Recognition

With the resumption of active work, the portfolio expands. Stations, involvements and distinctions:

  • —Co-production of a podcast, awarded the Comenius EduMedia Seal 2025
  • —Co-involvement in tour and performance management across Germany
  • —Airplay promotion through established radio contacts
  • —Base: Berlin
2026
fipsflake.com

After long deliberation, the decision was made to make the agency (partially) public. This reflects the passage of time and an expanding internal structure that is set to take new directions in the near future. For the first time, there is a public address — this website. It says who we are. But not who we work with. That remains, as always, our secret.
